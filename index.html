How To Add Notes To Your Audio Recording:

1. Upload Audio File by pressing "Choose File" button (only mp3 files are accepted)

2. Press "Play Audio" button to display audio recording.

3. Play the audio recording and pause when you want to want to add notes to a specific timestamp.

4. Press "Open Text Editor" Button, which will display a textbox for you to input notes.

5. Press submit to print your notes along with the timepoint of the audio recording specified.
